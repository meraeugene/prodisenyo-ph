Narrative

The Meridian Atrium stands as a testament to the intersection of gravity and ambition.

Every angle is a deliberate dialogue between the ground plane and the skyline, engineered for a high-density urban environment.

Interior Central Void Study

Fig. 01 // Computational Analysis

PHASE_A.dwg

Perhaps the most striking feature is the triple-height atrium, which utilizes a tension-cable system to support its massive glass facade. This creates a transparent 'lung' for the building.

The cantilevered observation decks, protruding 15 meters from the facade on the 45th and 72nd floors, utilize bespoke dampers to manage aerodynamic vibrations, providing visitors with an unparalleled view.
